Omlet

Description

This is also great breakfast dish, fast and easy made

When making this meal, there are no boundaries for creativity when comes to flavours and design

Indgredients
  • eggs
  • cheese
  • salami
  • bacon
  • mushrooms
  • any other thing that come to your mind
Steps
  1. Fry the bacon, mushrooms, salami etc.
  2. Add eggs to the meal
  3. Wait for eggs to become almost done
  4. add cheese, bosiljak, or something else
